About Us

For the best in authentic, made-to-order Italian-style pizzas, pastas and subs, look no further than Carlo's Pizza! We have the pizzas and pastas that you're craving, as well as appetizers, salads, dinners, calzones and sandwiches.

Our hand-tossed pizzas are made fresh when you order them, and still hot from the oven when you pick them up or eat in the dining room. Our pastas are served with authentic sauces. Our heroes and subs are perfect for lunch or dinner, and don't forget your party guests by ordering our "by-the-foot" subs!

A family-owned and operated pizzeria, Carlo's Pizza proudly serves the central New Jersey area. Our sauces and recipes have been handed down for generations, and are what make us one of the best pizzerias around. You can taste the difference!

Since we are family owned and operated, we can choose ingredients that are fresh and tasty, and pile them on as you like them. We only choose the most flavorful meats and cheeses for your eating enjoyment. Made to order, when you order, you will get the hottest pizza, the biggest sub, the best meal you've ever had... you will leave full, we guarantee it!

We can also offer catering, group, party and game-day specials to make your experience more unique. Dine in, or pick up your party platters, pizzas, 6-foot subs and more. You let us know what you need and we'll pile the fixings on for you.

When you're hungry for authentic flavors, visit us at Carlo's Pizza!
